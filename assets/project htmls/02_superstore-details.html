Objective: Enhance Profitability and Optimize Operations for Global Superstore

  • A comprehensive deep-dive into the Global Superstore dataset was performed to identify underperforming areas, discover key drivers of profitability, and recommend data-backed strategies to improve sales and profit margins.

Analysis & Key Steps

  • Data Cleaning & Transformation: Utilized SQL and R to ensure data quality, handle missing values, and prepare the dataset for trend analysis.
  • Key Metric Analysis: Calculated and analyzed Revenue, Profit Ratio, and Sales across different dimensions (Regions, Segments, Categories, Ship Modes).
  • Visualization & Reporting: Developed an interactive, multi-view dashboard in Tableau to visualize sales trends, regional performance, and profit/loss by product category over time.
  • Time-Series Trend: Examined the Revenue Trend Over Time to assess the company's annual growth and stability.

Key Finding & Recommendation

  • Finding: The Technology category consistently generates the highest revenue, but certain regions and product segments Consumer show disproportionately lower profit ratios due to high shipping costs and aggressive discounting. The Home Office segment is the most profitable but currently accounts for the lowest sales volume.
  • Recommendation: Launch a targeted marketing campaign focused on increasing sales within the Home Office segment, while simultaneously reviewing the pricing and shipping strategy for low-profit, high-volume products in the Consumer segment to balance revenue and profit.
