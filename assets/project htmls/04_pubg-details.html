Objective: To analyze PUBG player data and uncover performance and gameplay patterns

  • Examining player performance metrics, identifying key factors affecting wins, and segmenting players based on playstyle and in-game behavior.

Analysis & Key Steps

  • Data Aggregation & Cleaning: Consolidated PUBG dataset with 6.4 million rows including match stats, kills, damage, and player rankings. Cleaned data to remove duplicates, handle missing values, and standardize metrics for analysis.
  • Player Segmentation: Grouped players into categories (e.g., "Pro," "Average," "Noob") based on metric damage dealt to understand skill distributions.
  • Performance Trend Analysis: Analyzed patterns such as peak play times, drop locations, weapon preferences, and survival rates to identify factors that influence success.
  • Visualization & Insights: Created dashboards in Tableau to compare player segments, highlight high-performing strategies, and visualize correlations between kills, damage, and win rates.

Key Finding & Recommendation

  • Finding: Players in the Average segment have higher survival rates but still struggle with consistent positioning and loot efficiency. Most wins are correlated with early-game positioning and damage management rather than total kills.
  • Recommendation: Focus training and tutorials on improving early-game strategies, efficient looting, and optimal landing spots. Insights can guide player improvement programs or inform game design adjustments for balancing competitive gameplay.
