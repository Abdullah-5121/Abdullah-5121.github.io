Objective: Converting Casual Riders to Annual Members

  • A thorough analysis of 12 months of Cyclistic bike-share data was conducted to identify key behavioral differences between annual members and casual riders, aiming to inform a new marketing strategy.

Analysis & Key Steps

  • Data Cleaning & Wrangling: Used R and RStudio to consolidate, clean, and pre-process.
  • Statistical Summary: Calculated average ride length and total rides to distinguish user types.
  • Visualization: Created dashboards in Tableau showing usage by day and month.

Key Finding & Recommendation

  • Finding: Casual riders use bikes significantly longer on weekends for leisure, while annual members use them for shorter, frequent commutes on weekdays.
  • Recommendation: Launch a promotional campaign targeting casual riders on weekends, emphasizing value and benefits for longer, leisurely rides, such as a "Weekend Warrior Annual Pass".
